About

01

I'm a data analyst currently working at GoCement, a supplier of cement and building materials for large-scale projects with major state-owned enterprises (BUMN) and other large clients across Indonesia, where I support business development through market research, pipeline analysis, and feasibility studies.

I recently completed my Master's degree in Information Technology Management at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), after finishing a Bachelor's in Telecommunication Engineering at Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya (PENS).

Most of my work has been built around BI and visualization tools — Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and Looker Studio — turning raw data into dashboards that non-technical teams can actually use. More recently, I've started directing AI (Claude, previously ChatGPT) as part of that workflow: automating reports, structuring analyses, and producing technical outputs without writing the code myself.

That mix — data analysis, visualization, and AI-directed problem-solving — is part of what's drawing me to Apple Developer Academy. I'd like to learn how to design and build the apps behind the data, not just analyze it.

Featured Projects

03

A look at how I've been combining data analysis with AI-directed workflows — and the process behind each one.

Due Diligence Automation

AI-Assisted Corporate Due Diligence

Evaluating new business partners means tracing company ownership through Indonesian legal entity documents (AHU). I designed a workflow where I direct Claude to read these documents, recursively trace shareholder structures across related companies, cross-check findings against public sources, and generate structured PDF reports — complete with ownership maps and risk matrices — using a consistent template refined across multiple companies.

Legal Documents → AI Shareholder Tracing → Risk Report (PDF)
My role: workflow design, document sourcing, accuracy review, report template standardization
Market Research

Market Intelligence for New Business Opportunities

When evaluating a new national program as a potential market opportunity, I defined the research questions and directed an AI-assisted research process to gather and synthesize information from multiple sources. The output: a comprehensive market intelligence report plus a condensed summary for leadership decision-making.

Research Scope → AI-Assisted Research → Intelligence Report
My role: scoping, directing research, fact-checking, executive summary writing
Reporting Automation

Sales Performance Reporting Automation

I designed a standardized Excel structure for analyzing regional sales performance, then built a repeatable process — directed through Claude — that turns that data into a polished, multi-page PDF report ready for regional and leadership audiences, including an anonymized version for sensitive presentations.

Raw Sales Data → Standardized Excel Model → Automated PDF Report
My role: data structure design, reporting logic, building a reusable template for each period
Feasibility Study

Feasibility Study for a Factory Partnership

For a cross-company manufacturing partnership project, I coordinated inputs from multiple stakeholders and iteratively built a multi-sheet Excel feasibility model covering several financing scenarios. Since I'm not from a finance background, I worked with Claude to translate the model's logic and outputs into plain language for non-finance stakeholders.

Partner Inputs → Financial Model → Plain-Language Summary
My role: stakeholder coordination, model structuring, plain-language communication

RFQ → Fulfillment → Invoice → Payment
